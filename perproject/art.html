Knight's a-Splendor

Another statue posed as a threesome, to showcase the unanimity with which soldiers fight. Here the horses in armour stand gaurd to protect & serve. An honor as always is ours!

Knights a Splendor was created with just two horse statues about 3 inches tall. The horses on either side are the same positioned in inverse. A tricky drawing in charcoal it is meant to portray solidarity and unity. The light thrown in stark contrast with not much middle ground.



Digital Waves

Digital Waves is my first foray into digital painting, I picked a subject I had never put paint to paper on and came about with facinating results owing to the details the medium afforded.

Waves caught in splendid detail, this is a digital perfection achieved with paintastaing hours bending over a tablet, to capture every stroke every wave every spray of water and freeze the moment in time.
