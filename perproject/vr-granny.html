VR Project

Project Specifications

Team Project: Dana Spillinger, Sherry Yang, Jialing Liu and Sharanya Sudhakar

Animation Team: Dana and Sherry Development and Effects Team: Jialing and Sharanya
Granny, the matriach of the orca JPod is here to teach you which fish to eat. Resident Orcas of the Pacific NorthWest only eat Salmon. In this experiance Granny will catch fish and feed you. When you eat the correct fish she will congradulate you.



Hip Hip Hooray- It Worked!

The Animations and assets are converted and imported for unity compatibility. Some of the moving plant life and fish are created using shader graph and vertex animations. The experiance is enhance by adding whale fins for the user controls. So the user can grab the fish with the whale fins to eat.
Bring in the assets and setting up the sequence of events to execute where the hardest. They expeiance had to be test and retested for accuracy and understanding.
Feedback on our experiance indicated that Granny "teaching" moment is not there. Other than this, the expriance has fun elements like burping sounds when the fish are eaten and the real live whale fin in Vr make it an enjoyable experiance.
