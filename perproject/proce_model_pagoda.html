Pagoda

Project Specifications

An Architechural model inspired by the the japanese pagoda. A level of detail has been added to this system, whereby the doors, the position of the doorknobs, the roof suports, the shingles in the roof, the railing, the floor height, the base and the stircase are all procedural systems that can be controlled reproduction.
